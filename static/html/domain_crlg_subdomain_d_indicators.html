Indicators

Total Population Population by age compiled from multiple sources (NSO, Eurostat & UN DESA) according to the country's preference
Child Population Child population (0-17 years) compiled from multiple sources (NSO, Eurostat & UN DESA)
Children a Share of the Total Population Percentage of children as a share of the total population compiled from multiple sources (NSO, Eurostat & UN DESA)
Number of Births Number of births (in thousands)
Total Fertility Rate Average number of live births a hypothetical cohort of women (aged 15-49 years) would have at the end of their reproductive period if they were subject during their whole lives to the fertility rates of a given period and if they were not subject to mortality
Net migration Net migration is the net total of migrants during that period, that is, the number of immigrants minus the number of emigrants, including both citizens and noncitizens.
